Mandarin Panda — Support

Learn Chinese vocabulary with a pixel pet companion. Mandarin Panda includes 3,500+ HSK words across 4 levels, a virtual pet that grows as you study, and home/lock screen widgets for quick review.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I study words?

Tap "Start Studying" on the home screen. You'll see a Chinese character with pinyin and English translation. Swipe right if you know it, left if you don't. Words you miss will appear again sooner.

How do I feed my pet?

Your pixel pet gains HP automatically as you study words correctly. The more you study, the healthier your pet stays. If you stop studying for too long, your pet's HP will gradually decrease.

What is the Revival Lantern?

The Revival Lantern is an optional in-app purchase ($19.99). If your pet's HP reaches zero, a Revival Lantern will automatically activate to restore 50% HP and preserve all your progress. You can buy multiple lanterns in advance from the Settings page. They stack and are consumed one at a time.

What happens when my pet dies?

If your pet's HP reaches zero and you don't have a Revival Lantern, you'll see a death screen with two options: purchase a Revival Lantern to save your pet, or tap "Start Over" to begin fresh. Starting over resets your pet but your word progress is preserved.

How do I use the widget?

Long-press your home screen, tap the "+" button in the top corner, search for "Mandarin Panda", and add either the home screen widget (shows a word to review) or the lock screen widget (compact word display). Widgets update periodically with words from your study list.

Does the app work offline?

Yes. All 3,500+ words are included with the app. No internet connection is required to study.

How does iCloud sync work?

If iCloud is enabled on your device, your study progress and pet status sync automatically across all your Apple devices signed into the same iCloud account.
