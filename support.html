FAQ

Q: What is Learn Mandarin Words?
A: A flashcard app that plays Chinese words like music. Swipe left to feed words to your pixel pet. The home screen widget repeats your words all day.

Q: How does the Revival Lantern work?
A: Buy it in Settings as insurance. When your pet's HP hits zero, it activates automatically and restores 50% health. All progress is kept.

Q: Do I need to pay?
A: No. All 3,500+ words are completely free. The Revival Lantern is purely optional — you can always restart for free.

Q: How do I add the widget?
A: Long-press your home screen → tap + → search the app name → add the widget. Feed some words and turn on Marathon mode first.

Q: How do I restore purchases?
A: Tap "Restore Purchases" at the bottom of the Settings page.
