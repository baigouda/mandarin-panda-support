Mandarin Panda — Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 18, 2026

Overview

Mandarin Panda ("the App") is a Chinese vocabulary learning app with a virtual pixel pet companion. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data the App collects, stores, and shares.

Data We Collect

Mandarin Panda does not collect any personal information. We do not require account creation, login, or any personally identifiable information to use the App.

Data Stored on Your Device

All vocabulary learning progress, pet status, badge achievements, and user preferences are stored locally on your device using Apple's UserDefaults and, if enabled, synced across your devices via iCloud Key-Value Store. This data never leaves Apple's ecosystem and is not accessible to us.

In-App Purchases

Mandarin Panda offers an optional consumable in-app purchase (Revival Lantern). All payment processing is handled entirely by Apple through StoreKit. We do not collect, store, or have access to any payment or billing information.

Analytics and Tracking

Mandarin Panda does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking frameworks. We do not track your usage, location, or any other personal data.

Children's Privacy

Mandarin Panda does not knowingly collect personal information from children. The App does not require or collect any personal data from users of any age.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
